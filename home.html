Our mission is to digitize the strawberry industry.

Less guesswork. More efficient farming.

Many strawberry farms across Australia (and around the world) rely on paper-based systems to capture information about the operation of their business. These processes are error prone, human-capital intensive, and inefficient.

Simply Farm lets you get back to what you love - working your farm - by taking the effort out of your regular paperwork and farm management activities.
